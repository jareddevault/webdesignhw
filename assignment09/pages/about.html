Chocolate Chip Cookie Instructable

  • Why did you choose this topic?

    I have been a hobby cook and baker for several years. One of the first recipes I wanted to develop to specifically suit my tastes was chocolate chip cookies.

  • Why should I listen to you?

    I have very limited experience working in kitchens; however, I spend hours researching and trying recipes in my own time. My personal connection to food I feel was what makes the difference. And as a bonus, I didn't make you read my life story before the recipe!

  • Are there any other recipes I should check out for Chocolate Chip Cookies?

    Claire Saffitz has one of the best cookie recipes I have ever tried, granted it is a bit more involved than mine.
