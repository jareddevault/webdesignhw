Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • .5 Cup White Sugar
  • 3/4 Cup Brown Sugar, Packed
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 Cup Butter
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 1/4 Cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 Tsp Baking Soda
  • 8 Oz. total of your choice of chocolate chips/chunks, or Chopped Chocolate (my preference)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F
  2. Beat together sugars, salt, and butter until smooth
  3. Whisk in egg and vanilla until well incorperated
  4. Sift in the flour and baking soda until just combined Do Not Overmix
  5. Stir in chopped chocolate, or the chips or chunks of your choice
  6. Bake for 8-10 minutes

Best served still warm!
