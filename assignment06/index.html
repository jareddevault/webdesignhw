Offroad Honda Grom Build

About the Build

This is an outline for a custom offroad build project of a Honda Grom, transforming it into a capable offroad machine for adventurous rides.

2022 Honda Grom by Talon Sei: The inspiration for my build.

Essential Modifications

  • Knobby Tires: Kenda Big Block tires will make for a much more off-road capable machine. Shinko Mobber tires would be a great alternative.
  • Skid Plate: A skid plate to protect the engine and undercarriage from rocks and debris.
  • Handguards: Equipped with durable handguards for hand protection during offroad rides.
  • LED Offroad Lights: Installed powerful LED lights for enhanced visibility on trails.
  • Tail Tidy Kit: A "tail tidy" removes the stock rear fender, making for a cleaner look.
  • Foot Pegs: Wider foot pegs to keep your feet in place on bumpy roads.

Power Mods

  • Camshaft: A replacement camshaft will increase availbe power.
  • Exhaust: A new exhaust will be required to compliment the camshaft.
  • Intake: A more effecient intake will also compliment the camshaft.
  • ECU Reflash: The final requirement to complete the power mods would be an ECU reflash.
