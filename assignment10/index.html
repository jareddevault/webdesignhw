Tres Leches Cake


Tres Leches Cake is a sponge cake soaked in three kinds of milk: evaporated milk, condensed milk, and whole milk. The cake is popular throughout Latin America.

Tres Leches cake originiated in Nicaragua, and is their national pastry. Historians typical cite a trade agreement signed by President Roosevelt in 1936. The agreement lead to importation of Wisconsin preserved dairy products to Nicaragua. Canned milk such as the sweetened condensed, and evaporated milks, used in this dessert became very popular and had a distinct effect on Nicaraguan cuisine.
